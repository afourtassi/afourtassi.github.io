I am currently Maître de Conférences (Assistant Professor with tenure) of computer science at Aix-Marseille University.

I am also a research fellow at the Institute of Language, Communication and the Brain (ILCB), of which I am also member of the governing board.

I am the founder and director of the Computational Communicative Development project-team (CoCoDev), where we study children's communcative development in natural, interactive, and multimodal conexts.

I have a background in Mathematics and Physics (Ecole Polytechnique, Paris), in Computational Cognitive Science (Ecole Normale Supérieure, Ulm), and in Experimental Psychology (Stanford University).
